ILD service providers

"In telecom, "ILD service providers" refers to companies that provide "International Long Distance" services, essentially allowing users to make phone calls across international borders, meaning they facilitate connectivity between different countries through their network infrastructure; essentially, they are companies licensed to provide international calling services International Long Distance. To introduce free competition, International Long Distance (ILD) service was opened for private players in April 2002 based on the recommendations of Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI)."
